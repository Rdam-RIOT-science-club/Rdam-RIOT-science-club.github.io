The R.I.O.T. Science Club is a scientific community providing training in Reproducible, Interpretable, Open, and Transparent Science. Funded at King's College London by Dr. Samuel Westwood and colleagues, the RIOT Science Club was recently brought to Rotterdam by Elisabet Blok and Lorenza Dall'Aglio. The Rotterdam RIOT Science Club is now part of both the RIOTS at KCL and of the Open Science Community Rotterdam (OSCR). The Rotterdam RIOTS seminars occur monthly and are open to scientists, students, clinicians, as well as the public. Lectures, practicals, and discussions on how we can do better, clearer, and more reproducible science are given.
